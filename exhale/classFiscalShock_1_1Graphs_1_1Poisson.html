class Poisson¶

Poisson disc sampling based on https://www.cs.ubc.ca/~rbridson/docs/bridson-siggraph07-poissondisk.pdf.

Every point generated is guaranted to be at least r or minDistance units away from any other point.

Making Delaunay and Voronoi from a Poisson distribution: 

Poisson distribution = new Poisson(minDistanceBetweenSamples, width, height);
Delaunay poissonTriangulation = new Delaunay(distribution.vertices);
Voronoi poissonVoronoi = poissonTriangulation.makeVoronoi();

float FiscalShock.Graphs.Poisson.minDistance

r: Given any point, samples are chosen from the spherical annulus, a donut whose inner radius is r and outer radius, 2r. The scalar for the outer radius could be altered.

All points created by the distribution are at least this many units away from any other point.
